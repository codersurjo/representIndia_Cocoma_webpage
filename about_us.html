Meet our Founder

Anil Mahato (Founder, Director)

Anil Mahato is a total digital nerd passionate to create a better world & opportunities by developing digital technologies & services.

In his 12 years+ long career in the field of marketing, content development & programming, he has helped over a dozen of big brands like Amazon Prime Video, T-series, Progetto Happiness, 3 Multi Channel Network (MCNs) and 200+ YouTube Channels and produced over 4,500+ videos, published 10,000+ videos on YouTube which gathered billions of views and drove millions of online fans.

Leaving job-offer of managing Official Channels of Prime Ministor Office India & Narendra Modi, he started his online enterprises - Langistan, vShowcards & Cocoma Digital.
